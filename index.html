CV Droid Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy for CV Droid, an image editing application developed by Future Labs. CV Droid respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information that you may provide while using our app.

Information Collection and Use

CV Droid does not collect any personally identifiable information or sensitive user data. We do not store or have access to your images or any other personal information.

Permissions

In order to provide the intended functionalities, CV Droid may request access to your device's storage or camera. This access is solely for the purpose of allowing you to edit images and is not used for any other purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your images and data within the app. However, please be aware that the security of digital information cannot be guaranteed entirely.
